New Upcoming Releases

House of Flame and Shadow by Sarah J.Maas

Expected Publication: January 30, 2024

House of Flame and Shadow is the third book in the Crescent City series. Bryce Quinlan continues her adventurous life, only wanting to live back in Midgard with her friends, family and lover. But now, stranded in a strange new world, she'll have to find her way home.

Just For The Summer by Abby Jimenez

Expected Publication: April 2, 2024

Justin is cursed, every woman he dates ends up finding their soulmate as soon as they break up. But now this information has spread all over the internet. When a woman, Emma messages him telling him she has the same problem. They come up with a plan: they'll date each other to break the curse and hopefully finally find their own soulmates. It's supposed to be a quick fling, but when Emma's toxic mother shows up causing chaos. They're suddenly navigating a lot more than they expected. Will their plan end up working or have a different outcome?

The Reaperence Of Rachel Price by Holly Jackson

Expected Publication: April 2,2024

From the same author as A Good Girl's Guide to Murder. Holly Jackson is back with a story about eighteen-year-old Bel, who has lived her whole in the shadows of her mother's disappearance, with her being the only witness. Rachel Price vanishes but Bel has no memory of how it happened. Bel wishes everyone would just move on but the Price family agreed to a true crime documentary about it. Bel just wants filming to be over, but then, Rachel Price reappears, and lives change forever.

The Familiar by Leigh Brudugo

Expected Publication: April 9, 2024

Luzia Cotado uses scraps of magic to survive in a shabby house as a scullion. But her mistress discovers Luzia hiding her talent and demands Luzia use those gifts for the family's social position. It takes a turn when Luzia gets the attention of Antonio Pérez, the disgraced secretary to Spain's King reeling fom the defeat of his armada. Since the king is desperate for any advantage during the war against England's queen, Pérez will stop at nothing to regain the king's favor. Luzia is determined to seize this chance to better her fortunes so, she plunges into an entirely new world for her. Luzia with secrets of her own will try every to survive usin every bit of her wit.

Funny Story by Emily Henry

Expected Publication: April 23, 2024

Daphne begins her new story. Stranded in beautiful Waning Bay, Michigan, without her friends or family, right after her ex-fiance, Peter, realized he was in love with his childhood best-friend Petra. But at least Daphne has her dream job, as a children's librarian. Daphne attempts to propose being roommates with the one person who understands what she could be going through: Petra's ex, Miles Nowak.
